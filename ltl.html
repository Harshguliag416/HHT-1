Less Than Truckload (LTL)

Cost-Efficient Shared Capacity for Small and Mid-Size Shipments

Less Than Truckload (LTL) service enables efficient transportation of smaller freight by consolidating multiple shipments into a single truck, optimizing cost while maintaining reliable delivery across nationwide routes.

Service Fit

Best suited for flexible and cost-efficient shared freight movement

  • Shipments that do not require full trailer capacity
  • Recurring or smaller freight volumes optimized through consolidation
  • Cost-sensitive logistics where shared transport improves efficiency

Shipment Classification

02

Load Consolidation

Final Mile Delivery

Shipments are separated and delivered to final destinations with confirmation and communication.
