Our Expertise

Comprehensive Freight Solutions

We offer a full suite of logistics services to move your goods efficiently across the country, no matter the size or destination.

Full Truckload (FTL)

When your shipment is large enough to fill an entire trailer, our FTL service is the most cost-effective and efficient solution. We provide a dedicated truck and driver for your goods, ensuring a direct route from origin to destination with no intermediate stops or transfers. This minimizes handling, reduces the risk of damage, and guarantees the fastest possible transit time. Ideal for high-value, time-sensitive, or large-volume cargo.

Less Than Truckload (LTL)

Our LTL service is perfect for shipments that don't require a full trailer. We consolidate your freight with other shipments heading in the same direction, allowing you to share the cost of transportation. With a robust network of terminals and partners, we ensure your goods are handled efficiently and delivered on a reliable schedule. It's the smart, economical way to move smaller quantities of freight across the nation.

Drayage Services

Drayage is the critical first and last mile of your supply chain. Our specialized drayage services provide rapid and reliable transport of shipping containers between major US ports, rail terminals, and your distribution centers. We manage the complex port logistics, scheduling, and equipment to prevent delays and demurrage charges, ensuring your intermodal shipments transition smoothly and stay on schedule.

Port-to-Port Transport

Moving goods from a port to an inland destination requires expert coordination. Our port-to-port service is an integrated logistics solution that manages the entire journey of your containerized freight. From vessel unloading and drayage to rail or FTL/LTL transfer, we handle every handoff. This comprehensive oversight ensures complete visibility, security, and on-time delivery from the port of entry directly to your final destination.

Who We Are

About HHT Freight Group

Founded in 2025, HHT Freight Group is a premier third-party logistics (3PL) provider, expertly managing freight across the 48 contiguous United States from our India-based control center. We were built on the principle that modern logistics requires both global oversight and local expertise. Our unique operational model allows us to provide round-the-clock support and coordination, while our strong network of US-based carrier partners ensures your freight moves safely, on-time, and with full regulatory compliance. Our core mission is to deliver reliable, scalable, and transparent transportation solutions that empower our clients' supply chains.
